Delete Account & Data

Last updated: July 17, 2026

How to Delete Your Account

To request deletion of your account and associated data from Rameswaram Dry Fish, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Rameswaram Dry Fish app on your device
  2. Go to Profile tab
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Alternative Method (Email)

If you cannot access the app, send an email from your registered email address to:

rameswaramdryfish@gmail.com

with the subject line: "Delete Account Request"

Data Deletion Details

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • Saved delivery addresses
  • Order history
  • Preferences and settings

Data Retention

The following data may be retained for legal and accounting purposes:

  • Transaction records for tax compliance (retained for 7 years as per Indian tax laws)
  • Anonymized analytics data (no longer linked to your identity)

Processing Time

Your deletion request will be processed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.
